About

I'm a Full-Stack Developer from Las Vegas, Nevada. I spent 3 years at the University of Las Nevada majoring in Mechanical Engineering where I developed problem solving skills. Near the end I found myself wanting to pursue a different career. This is when I took my first coding course with C++. I fell in love the language and the idea of being able to create something that others could one day find useful and enjoy!

This is where I then decided to fast-track my career and embark on my six month journey with AppAcademy. With the fast and rigorous curriculum I've learned to adapt to a fast changing environment where one week I would learning React and then the next learning about Python. I found myself to really enjoy the fast pace and also being able to work with other future developers for various projects. I look forward to continuing down this path and creating many more projects along the way!
